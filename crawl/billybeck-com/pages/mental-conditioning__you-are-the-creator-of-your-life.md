---# You Are The Creator Of Your Life

Lions & Lionesses! 🦁🦁🦁

🏆One of the greatest secrets of life is that we are NOT victims of our life circumstance.

🦁We are the CREATORS of our LIFE circumstance.

This isn’t a theory.

This is the Science of Life. 🧬

As you explore and contemplate life you will discover this for yourself. I’m sure most of you already know this!

But put most simply….

Your thoughts/beliefs create your feelings.

Your feelings and emotions create your actions/inactions.

Add all of your actions/inactions up and that is the place where you stand today!

Know this and you have the Power to Create what you desire.

But first, you must decide what you want!

Every day and as often as you can deliberately ask yourself:

✅ “What do I want to create and how can I enjoy every moment of this awesome journey?”

✅ Write it out!

✅ Speak it out loud!

✅ Record your voice and listen to it any time you feel disconnected or stuck!

✅ Own it!

What you feed grows! 🦁🦁🦁

✅What you think about with passion and intensity EXPANDS!

It gains POWER & STRENGTH!!!

You were born a LION…not a lamb.

You are here to CREATE & SERVE…To LEAD, not follow.
