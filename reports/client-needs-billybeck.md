# Client Needs Brief — Billy Beck III

**Client:** Billy Beck III
**Brand:** billybeck.com / Legion of Lions
**Compiled from:** Onboarding (Apr 03), Discovery (Mar 24), Strategy & Site Review (May 02)

---

## 1. Strategic Vision

The site must shift away from a generic "personal trainer / gym / get-ripped" identity and toward a **transformation-through-self-mastery** brand. Fitness is the *vehicle*, not the offer. The offer is identity change — the **Lion vs. Lamb** metaphor — taught through training, nutrition, and mental conditioning.

**Core thesis:** *"Where the mind goes, the body follows."* The body is the proving ground for self-mastery; the lasting outcome is a heart-driven leader who can serve, contribute, and lead without burning out.

**5-year goal:** build the **Legion of Lions** community to 1,000 members first, then scale toward 33,000+ as the recurring-revenue spine. Coaching becomes the upsell, not the front door.

---

## 2. Audience & Anti-Audience

### Dream customer
- Heart-driven leaders, entrepreneurs, Tony Robbins / Platinum Partner archetype
- "Committed achievers" usually 40+ entering the magician archetype phase
- Want to transform body **and** mind; reject hustle-burnout culture
- Believe they can sculpt their life; willing to do the work

### Anti-audience / messaging to avoid
- "You're not enough unless you have a six-pack" — explicitly reject this
- Shortcut culture, hack culture, body-shaming
- Generic trainer / gym vibes
- Profanity in any AI/clone output

---

## 3. Site Information Architecture

The site needs the following pages, each tuned to a specific role in the funnel:

| Page | Purpose | Notes |
|------|---------|-------|
| Homepage | Drive movement + impact + offer | Lead with Lion-vs-Lamb story, not products |
| Legion of Lions (money page) | Primary $97/mo offer | Replaces / re-frames the BB3 Blueprint |
| Coaching | Waitlist only | Position exclusivity: "spots closed, join waitlist" |
| About — Billy Beck III | Founder / philosophy | Three Pillars, Eight Habits, credentials |
| Testimonials | Social proof | Side-by-side before/after; app screenshots |
| Blog | SEO / AEO content engine | Auto-posted from AI Copywriter pipeline |
| Contact | Direct line | Already restyled; keep current treatment |

**Out of scope for now:** the Billy Beck III brand and Legion of Lions remain unified under billybeck.com. The legionalliance.com domain is held for future separation.

---

## 4. Homepage — Required Sections

1. **Hero** — bold typographic statement of the movement; not a fitness CTA.
2. **Lion vs. Lamb story** — the core philosophy in a scannable section.
3. **Featured testimonial / before-after** — high-quality, real, no AI-faked imagery.
4. **Impact section** — charity, food bank, anti-trafficking work, framed as the *movement* not as self-promotion.
5. **Live Impact Counter** — meals provided, children helped. Counter must increment on book/membership purchases.
6. **Three Pillars** — Physical Training · Effective Eating · Mental Conditioning.
7. **Coaching teaser** → click out to waitlist (not a hard sell).
8. **Lead magnet** — book opt-in (~$7) that funds ~28 meals per purchase. New shorter "blueprint" booklet replaces the current 7-pillar PDF.
9. **CTA → Legion of Lions** — primary conversion target.

## 6. Offer Architecture

| Tier | Price | Limit | Role |
|------|-------|-------|------|
| Legion of Lions (membership) | $97 / month or $997 / year | Unlimited | Front door, recurring revenue, community |
| Group Coaching (BB3 app) | $6,000 / 6 months · $10,000 / year | ~100 active | Mid-tier, waitlist-driven |
| 1-on-1 Coaching | ~$5,000 / month | Few seats | Premium, referral-driven |
| Elite (Tony-level clients) | 6 figures+ | Handful | Off-site, not promoted |
| Lead magnet booklet | ~$7 | n/a | Funds meals; impact-counter trigger |

**Funnel logic:** all marketing drives to Legion of Lions. Coaching is presented as a waitlist. The booklet is a low-friction first transaction that doubles as a charitable contribution.

---

## 7. Brand Voice & Sacred Phrases

**Voice:** transformative, authentic, courageous, decisive. Sincere over slick. "If I can do it, you can do it" — not "look how impressive I am."

**Sacred phrases to use:**
- "Live like a lion, not a fearful lamb."
- "Challenge creates change."
- "Where the mind goes, the body follows."
- "Victory" — used as an anchor in client comms.
- "Devoted, humble student of self-mastery."

**Phrases to avoid:**
- Any profanity
- "Get ripped," "shred," "transform your body in 30 days"
- "You're not enough unless..."

## 9. Constraints & Non-Negotiables

- **No AI-faked photography** of transformations. Real before/after only.
- **Don't replace the existing public site without involving Simon** (the current web maintainer treats it as his own); stage the new build in parallel.
- **Don't overwrite the WordPress page from REST without regenerating Elementor cache** — the editor will look fine while the frontend stays stale.
- **No `html` widgets** in any Elementor layout pushed via REST — frontend renders break.
- **Staging-first** for every CRM / automation change — never point new flows at production until verified.
- **Andrea's bandwidth is limited** (two toddlers); systems must be operable by her with one VA assistant. No tooling that requires Billy in the loop daily.

---

## 10. Success Criteria

The site is "done" when:

1. A first-time visitor understands within 10 seconds that this is a movement about self-mastery, not a fitness program.
2. The homepage drives to Legion of Lions with a single, unambiguous CTA.
3. Every transactional moment (book purchase, membership, donation) updates the live impact counter.
4. Coaching is positioned as exclusive / waitlisted, not actively sold.
5. Andrea can publish a new blog post, send a campaign, and read meaningful funnel data without involving Billy.
6. The brand voice on the site, in emails, and from the AI clone is indistinguishable from Billy's lion-vs-lamb teaching.
